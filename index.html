BREAKING: The Slovak Legend of "Bryndzové halušky" Could Be Real

The Ankh-Morporkean explorer Rincewind has found evidence of "Bryndzové halušky" in the Slovak village of "Košice". This legendary dish is rumored to be a source of calcium, as well as a wide variety of healthy proteins.

Health benefits include strong bones, healthy fats, strong muscles, and more. Some stories hint about immortality, similar to that of Ambrosia.

Ankh-Morporkean cooks were unable to replicate this dish. They failed to obtain "Bryndza", an integral component, made out of sheep milk, according to locals. Slovakia and surrounding regions still remain as the only hotspots in which this dish has been spotted.

Bryndzové halušky ingredients
1. "Halušky"
2. "Bryndza"
3. Bacon
