The "Octagon", Finally Discovered

Yesterday, world famous explorer Jack Black has finally discovered what he calls the "Octagon". Jack had been searching for this elusive shape since he was only 20 years old.

Why did he do it? He said, "For generations, my family has been in search for the "Octagon". None of my ancestors were successful, however, I am the first in the family to actually find the shape." It's likely Jack will soon win a Nobel Prize for this discovery.
